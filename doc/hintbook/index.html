Steve's Hints

Welcome to the official Hintbook!
What am I supposed to do?

You'll need to flick the old switch back. To do that, you'll have to clean and re-assemble that parts and produce some electricity.

Who are all these people?

In general, just try to help people out. Below, there's pictures of some of these people. Click them to get some hints on how to help them.

What's with the shadows?

The three main store owners all got torn apart. Help out their cute and horror counterparts to restore them!

Shadows are gone, now what?

Jim (top floor) will clean the parts for you, Billie (bottom floor) will fix the switch. Then, you can install the switch and talk to Steve-E-Wonder to get the thing powered.

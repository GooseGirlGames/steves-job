Steve's Hints

Steve-E's Music Shop

Help out Cute-E-Wonder and Steve-E-Horror to restore Steve-E-Wonder's Music Shop and generator.

Cute: Cute-E-Horror

Small Hint

There's a guy in Steve-E-Horror's store who's about to get rid of his spine.

Medium Hint

Greasing the torture device will help it work. Godd thing there's a vending machine for that!

Big Spoiler

Enter the Cute Mall and join the amazing Cult of Goose to obtain a goose. In the Cute Mall, use a Quarter and the Goose in the Geese-to-Grease machine to obtain the grease.

Horror: Steve-E-Horror

Small Hint

When you're done fixing Steve-E-Horror's problem, you can enter his store to grab a bucket.

Medium Hint

His speech is a little strange (or a "wittle stwange", perhaps). There's been some talk about aquatious translation devices recently.

Big Spoiler

Use a coin on the Aquaclaw to get a Babelfish, then give it to Steve-E-Horror.

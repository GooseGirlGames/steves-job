Steve's Hints

Billie's Repair Shop

Help out Kim and Hangman to restore Billie Bauhaus's Repair Shop.

Cute: Kim

Small Hint

Actually, help out that cute little raccoon next to Kim first, if you don't mind.

Medium Hint

Kim's Candy Cane can be plugged into a thing nearby.

Big Spoiler

Use the Candy Cane to retract the marquee above the Cult of Goose. This'll allow you to pour stuff onto these poor people.

Horror: Hangman

Small Hint

A magpie can fetch those shiny keys for you.

Medium Hint

Trouble getting the keys from the magpie? The raccoon will help you.

Big Spoiler

Fred needs his apron cleaned. We've forgot which, but either Jimmy the Cat or Laundr-O-Man will help. You'll get a cocktail that the raccoon will appreciate.
